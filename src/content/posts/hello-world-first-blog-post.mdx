Hello, world!

I've been a Flutter developer for a while now, building apps and diving deep into how things work. This blog is where I'll share what I learn.

## Why Start a Blog?

Writing helps me think. When I explain something, I understand it better. And if my explanations help someone else, even better.

## What to Expect

I'll write about:

- **Flutter internals** — How things actually work under the hood
- **Architecture patterns** — Practical approaches to building maintainable apps
- **Dart language features** — Making the most of the language
- **Mobile development** — Beyond just Flutter

## My Philosophy

I believe in:

- **Simplicity** — Don't over-engineer
- **Understanding fundamentals** — Know why, not just how
- **Learning in public** — Share the journey, not just the destination

## Let's Go

Thanks for reading. I'm excited to start this journey.
